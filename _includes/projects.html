Manitoba Association of Foodbanks

A PHP-based web application designed to solve the issue of the foodbanks needing to monitor community statistics for their yearly report.

A web application was chosen due to the ease at which you can access the contents. With a central server, the staff can login using their smartphone or any other internet device at any time, whereas other solutions may have had the individual foodbanks buy expensive technologies to store data.
